▸ How it works · Score formula · Trading signals

v6 Score Rebalancing (L1-aware): Score weights re-tuned for L1 data feeds. Volume Pressure dropped to 10% (was 30%) because the bid/ask position proxy was unreliable on L1 due to potentially-stale snapshots. Relative Volume and Price vs EMA boosted to 26% each (the most reliable L1-derived signals). Volatility Expansion and Options Activity now 19% each.

v6 Volume Pressure Rewrite: The VP component no longer uses the bid/ask position proxy. Instead it tracks tick-direction (last price up/down vs previous poll) volume-weighted over a 30-second sliding window. Cleaner signal on L1 because it relies only on the last field, which is the most reliable L1-tier data point.

v6 Don't-Trade Warning: Prominent red banner appears when regime is low-vol or choppy AND no candidate has lasted >30 seconds in the last hour. Helps you recognize untradable days early instead of refreshing through dead market hours.

v6 Low-Vol Override: On low-vol days, the close-window thresholds no longer loosen. Instead, midday-strict thresholds apply throughout the close window because late-day flickers in low-vol environments are even less reliable than usual.

v6 Bug Fixes: Momentum Type now tags candidates at promotion time (was tagging only after demotion in v5). Breakout detector memory bloat fix for long sessions.

v5 Time-of-Day Tuning: Thresholds auto-adapt based on market time. Strictest at the 9:30 open (lots of noise), default midday, loosest in the 3:00-4:00 close window (catches late-day moves). Disable in Settings if you prefer manual control.

v5 Acceleration Section: A new third tier appears above Candidates during the 3:00-4:00 PM close window. Shows tickers with active consolidation breakout patterns (last 5 min range > 1.5x the previous 25 min, with price breaking out). These are late-day moves that wouldn't qualify under standard criteria but are tradable with tight exits.

v5 Regime Indicator: Banner at the top labels today's market (trending/choppy/low-vol) based on SPY behavior over the last 30 min. Add SPY to your scan for this to activate. On choppy days, stability requirements auto-tighten.

v5 Momentum Type (Type column): DRIFT steady climb, ideal for OTM options · THRUST sharp short move, risky entry · REVR direction flip, high risk/reward.

v5 Breakout Column (Brk): "BRK" badge when consolidation breakout is active. Number (0-100) shown when building but not yet triggered. Value ≥ 60 with price outside range = active.

v5 Bulletproof Persistence: Every transition and poll is saved to localStorage immediately. A mid-session refresh or accidental tab close will resume exactly where you left off. A heartbeat warns if a long outage is detected.

v5 Session Summary: Click "Session Summary" in the footer (or at end of session) to generate a one-page HTML report with top candidates, flip stats, momentum-type breakdown, and regime analysis. Printable / save as PDF.

v4 Stability · Streak · Flips: Tickers must qualify in 8/10 cycles AND hold direction for 60s before promoting. Streak column = current qualifying duration. Flips column = transitions today (high = choppy, avoid). Warning icon appears next to ticker name if flip count exceeds threshold.

v6 Score components (default weights): Volume Pressure 10% · Relative Volume 26% · Price vs EMA 26% · Volatility Expansion 19% · Options Activity 19%.
