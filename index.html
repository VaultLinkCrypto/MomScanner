▸ How it works · Score formula · Trading signals

v4 Stability Filter: A ticker must qualify as candidate for at least N of the last M poll cycles AND remain in the same direction continuously for the minimum streak duration before it promotes to the Candidates list. This prevents the rapid bull/bear flipping seen on choppy days. Tune the thresholds in Settings → Stability Filter.

Streak column: shows how long this ticker has been continuously qualifying in its current direction. Reset to 0 any time it fails a criterion or reverses direction. Trade when Streak > 1:00.

Flips column: count of state transitions today. Low = stable trend ticker. High = choppy. Colored orange when approaching warn threshold, red when exceeding it. Avoid trading tickers with high flip counts regardless of current dot color.

Session Log: every state transition is recorded below with full context (score, price, spread, RelVol). Review after each session to learn which setups worked and which were traps. Export as CSV for analysis.

Action dots: Solid green/red = stable candidate (passed stability filter). Hollow = tracking. Dashed+faded = warming up (<3 min, unreliable).

Score components: Volume Pressure (30%) uptick/downtick proxy · Relative Volume (20%) vs 20-day avg · Price vs EMA (20%) ATR-normalized · Volatility Expansion (15%) range vs ATR · Options Activity (15%) spread + OI quality
