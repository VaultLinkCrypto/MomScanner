How this tool works · How the score is computed

Workflow: Paste tickers from your thinkorswim scan below. The tool polls your chosen data feed (Tradier or Schwab) on an adaptive interval. Each ticker gets a raw score computed from 5 weighted components. That raw score is smoothed with an EMA so it drifts instead of jumping. Direction arrow and Cumulative Score Change update in real time (no smoothing).

The 5 score components:

• Volume Pressure — proxy for buy vs sell volume using uptick/downtick proxy (last trade vs bid/ask midpoint).
• Relative Volume — today's cumulative volume vs 20-day average, time-of-day adjusted.
• Price vs EMA — distance from 9-period EMA, normalized by ATR(14) so a $15 stock and a $500 stock are comparable.
• Volatility Expansion — current session range vs ATR(14), catches breakouts from consolidation.
• Options Activity — proxy based on today's options volume vs open interest where available.

Raw score ranges 0–100; 50 = balanced. Extreme readings (>75 or <25) indicate strong directional probability. The arrow and Cumulative columns update every tick; the main Score column is smoothed over your configured lag window.
